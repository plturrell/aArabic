## Core Process Types

### 1. PO Invoices (Standard Process)
- Processed via eProcurement system
- Requires VAT classification and Purpose of Payment (POP)
- Most common payment type

### 2. Manual Invoices (Non-PO)
- Exceptional basis only
- Requires 3 approvals: Cost Centre Owner, Supply Chain Manager, CFO
- Processed centrally by APU team

### 3. Manual Payments (In-Country)
- **One-off:** Requires MD/CEO + CFO approval
- **Recurring:** Requires dispensation + CFO approval
- Paid through payment suspense account
- Must be recorded in eOPS

### 4. Cross Border Recharges (CBR)
- Managed centrally by GBS APU CBR team
- No local processing required

---

## Key Controls

### Mandatory Approvals
- Cost Centre Owner (all non-PO payments)
- Supply Chain Manager (manual invoices/payments)
- CFO/Delegate (all non-PO payments)
- MD/CEO (one-off manual payments only)

### Payment Verification
- Vendor verification (onboarded or screened)
- Amount within DOA limits (with documented exceptions)
- Payment to correct vendor
- 48-hour rule for screening validity

### Documentation
- Proper VAT classification (11 categories)
- Purpose of Payment (POP)
- All approvals documented
- Dispensation for recurring payments

---

## Critical Compliance Points

### ⚠️ High Risk Areas
1. **Fraud Prevention:** Verify all approvals and vendor details
2. **Duplicate Payments:** Check invoice numbers and PO numbers
3. **DOA Compliance:** Ensure amounts within limits (exceptions: GSAM legal fees, Tax/VAT)

### ⚠️ Common Failures
- Missing approvals
- Expired screening (>48 hours)
- Missing dispensation for recurring payments
- Incorrect VAT classification
- Not clearing payment suspense accounts monthly

## Payment Suspense Account Management

### Accounts
- **BHD:** 09-0906743-50
- **USD:** 09-0906743-50

### Process
1. Extract PSGL (140-288843-800)
2. Reconcile to trial balance
3. Extract eBBS transactions
4. Compare and clear via PSGL entry

### Requirement
- **All items cleared in same month**
- Aged items escalated per grid

## VAT Classification Summary

| Category | Use Case |
|----------|----------|
| Full Tax Invoice | Standard compliant invoices |
| Simplified Tax Invoice | Amounts ≤ BHD 500 |
| Zero Rated | Transport, food, healthcare, education, construction, oil/gas |
| Exempt | Real estate, financial services |
| Reverse Charge | Overseas suppliers (calculate VAT) |
| Out of Scope | Pre-2019 supplies, MPVs |
